❯ let's start a new project next to board-games-agent, mage-knight-player-aid, etc...
  project name: milky-way
  type: a board game contains dice rolling yahtzee style and push your luck
  
  setup:
  6 cards as a path

  rules:
  each player rolls 6 dice on start of their turn
  must decide to keep at least 1 kind of product from the dice results: banana, cacao, strewberry, grapefruit, carrot.
  now he may choose: 1. keep the amount of product he rolled and keep them, giving to all player the rest.
  2. continue to roll all different kind of dice result but this time must discard 1 die and give to other players the result.
  
  doing that for the rest of the 5 cards, 6 in total in a round. while each card specify how many dice they must discard on each step (card)
  
  cards: 1 - keep all. 2 - keep 4 discard 2. 3 - keep 2 discard 4. 4 - keep 5, discard 1. 5 - keep 4 discard 2. 6 - keep 3 discard 3.
  
  scoring:
  each player sum his score for each fruit every round, by what he collected as an active player and gain by opponents discard.
  the winner is the player who won the majority on th most projects after 6 rounds.
